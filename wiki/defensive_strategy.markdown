# DEFENSIVE STRATEGY

* Zero-trust systems: Isolated units, low surface area, self-reconcilication in
  the face of bad peripherals.
    * Questions: Can I depend on my inputs? How trust-worthy are they?
    * Degrade gracefully: Do only as much as you can trust.
* Web of Trust: Reputation can be bought, sold and verified.
* Abstract out important things to third-parties: Stripe, Google Auth, etc. Make
  yourself unattractive.

## Questions

* There is not Anti-memetics division: You have to figure things out, reassemble
  yourself from nothing. Who am I? Should not be an important question, goals
  need to be reassessed from first principals in a clean-room way.
* Accelerando: What can we learn about where the future is going based on ideas
  that already exist? The things predicted 20 years ago are starting to show up
  now.
