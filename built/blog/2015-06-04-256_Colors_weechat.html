  1. 1. Setup your term variable because you use screem or tmux and it's wrong and
weechat doesn't understand because software.

    # Older weechat:
    /shell setenv TERM=screen-256color
    /upgrade
    # Newer weechat
    /set env TERM screen-256color
    /upgrade
