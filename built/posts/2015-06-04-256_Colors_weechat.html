256 Colors in weechat-curses

2015-06-04 by Quinlan Pfiffer

I'm sick of googling it everytime so I'm just going to put this here.

  1. Setup your term variable because you use screem or tmux and it's wrong and weechat doesn't understand because software.
    # Older weechat:
    /shell setenv TERM=screen-256color
    /upgrade
    # Newer weechat
    /set env TERM screen-256color
    /upgrade
